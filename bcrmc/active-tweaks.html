BCR-MC 5
1.20: Trails & Tales

Currently Active Tweaks

Crafting Tweaks

  • Back to Blocks
    Slabs and stairs can be crafted back into blocks, with some loss.
  • Dropper to Dispenser
    Allows crafting a dropper together with a bow to create a dispenser.
  • Rotten Flesh to Leather
    Smelting rotten flesh in a furnace will yield one leather.
  • Charcoal to Black Dye
    Black dye can be crafted from charcoal.
  • Sandstone Dyeing
    Sandstone can be crafted together with red dye to make Red Sandstone.
  • Universal Dyeing
    Dyed blocks can be re-dyed to another. (And can be mixed in the recipe!)
  • Powder to Glass
    Smelting concrete powder will yield stained glass of the same color.
  • More Bricks
    Crafting four bricks in a square will yield four brick blocks instead of one.
  • More Bark
    Crafting four logs in a square will yield four bark blocks instead of one.
  • Craftable Name Tags
    Name tags are now craftable.
  • Unpackable Wool
    Wool blocks can be crafted into four string.

Other Tweaks

  • Fast Leaf Decay
    Naturally generated leaves will decay much faster once its logs are chopped down.
  • Custom Nether Portals
    Allows nether portals to be lit in any size and shape.
  • Durability Ping
    Sends you a chat message if your tool is below 10% durability. Can be customized or disabled with /trigger duraPing.
  • Cauldron Concrete
    Dropping concrete powder into a cauldron full of water will transform it into concrete.
  • Armored Elytra
    Drop an elytra and a chestplate onto an anvil to combine them. Do the same onto a grindstone to separate them.
  • Player Head Drops
    Killed players will drop their head as a block.
  • Double Shulker Shells
    Shulkers drop two shells.
  • Bat Membranes
    Disables phantoms and makes bats drop membranes instead.
  • More Mob Heads
    Mobs have a chance to drop their head as a block upon being killed.
  • Name Colors
    Name color customization. Use /trigger color to customize.
